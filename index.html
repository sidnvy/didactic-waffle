About Osaka Urban Development

Our Legacy

Since our founding in 1950, Osaka Urban Development Co., Ltd. has been instrumental in shaping the Kansai region's urban landscape. Our deep understanding of Osaka's unique culture and economic dynamics allows us to create spaces that not only meet the needs of today but anticipate the demands of tomorrow.

From the iconic Namba Parks to the cutting-edge Osaka Innovation Hub, our projects stand as testaments to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community enhancement.
